Calm Corner

Identity Design

  The Calm Corner is a drop-in space at NYU Shanghai for students to take a break, relax, and calm their minds. The space provides resources for mindfulness and meditation practices, breathing and stretching exercises, music playlists, expressive art, and guided journaling.

As I member of the Student Health Center I was responsible for the identity design of the Calm Corner space - crafting both digital and print assets.

Identity

In the sprawling urban landscape that is Shanghai - the Calm Corner is designed as an oasis for overwhelmed and restless students, reflected with natural imagery and soft, muted colors. Informative posters guide students on different meditative exercises and experiences.

Digital and Print. 2019.
