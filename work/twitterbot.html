Museum of Tweets

twitter bot

Tweets by tweetmuseum_bot

  The Tweet Museum bot is inspired by the users of Twitter, and wants to make art for them. With its crowd-sourced work, the bot creates a museum, hosted on Twitter.

  This bot is meant as a fun supplement to Twitter, by giving users unsolicited, bizarre, AI-generated works of "art". Created with Node.js and Processing, communicating with Twitter & Google Images APIs
