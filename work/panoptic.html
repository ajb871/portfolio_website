Panoptic

Interactive Installation

  Panoptic is an survielling installation, meant to investigate our relationships with the cameras that record us, and our relationship with the footage of those cameras. Though one may not always be conscious of it, in public they are the subject of some image or video almost constantly. Even with the vague awareness of being recorded, the specific details of the recordings, such as where the data is being stored, how long it exists before deletion, and the specific content of the video, is unbeknown to us.

Mimicking this, Panoptic will open its eye, alerting those present that one of the several cameras are recording. This footage is saved, and added to Panoptic's collection of data. Through the randomization of recording, saving, replaying, and deleting of videos, this piece imitates to imitate the confusing and unsettling presence of surveillance.

This piece was created in Max, communicating with 3 webcams individually.

Programmed in Max. 2018.
