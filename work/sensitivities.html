Sensitivities

Installation & Physical Computing

  Sensitivities is a Kinect sculpture created of reactive and perceptive flowers. Inspired by the mimosa pudica, a flower that can react to physical touch, this sculpture meditates on the space around it. The thin, delicate flowers physically react to their surroundings, and will withdraw into themselves if threatened by an over stimulation of people or motion.
When left alone, they breath gently.

This piece was created in Max, where input from a Kinect 2 is utilized to control multiple servos independently using an Arduino. The servos control the flowers' movement using a laser-cut scotch yoke mechanism.

Programmed in Max. Flowers fabricated with wood, paper, and wire. 2018.
